Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SkillConnect

How do I create an account on SkillConnect?

Creating an account is simple and free! Click the "Get Started" button, fill in your basic information including name, email, and phone number. You can then complete your profile by adding your skills, experience, and job preferences. The entire process takes less than 5 minutes.

Are all job postings verified and legitimate?

Yes, absolutely! We have a strict verification process for all companies and job postings. Our team manually reviews each job listing to ensure authenticity, verify company details, and prevent spam or fraudulent postings. We also collect feedback from job seekers to maintain quality standards.

Is SkillConnect completely free to use?

Yes, all basic features including job search, profile creation, and job applications are completely free. We also offer SkillConnect Premium with additional benefits like priority support, advanced analytics, resume optimization, and exclusive job opportunities for a small monthly fee.

How can I improve my chances of getting hired?

To maximize your success: (1) Complete your profile 100% with a professional photo, (2) Write a compelling summary highlighting your key achievements, (3) Keep your skills updated and relevant, (4) Apply to jobs regularly and promptly, (5) Use our resume builder tool, and (6) Enable job alerts to never miss opportunities.

How does the job alert system work?

Our smart job alert system sends you personalized notifications via email and SMS when new jobs matching your preferences are posted. You can set alerts based on job title, location, salary range, company size, and industry. Alerts are sent in real-time, daily, or weekly based on your preference.
